About My Company

Founded in 2017, My Company is committed to creating web experiences that are inclusive, fast, and discoverable. We believe the internet should work for everyone, no matter their device or ability.

From accessibility audits to full website redesigns, our team combines creativity with compliance to help small businesses stand out online.
